Story format — use this for every story
S
Situation: Context in 2 sentences. What was the system, what was the scale, what was the team?
T
Task: What specifically was your responsibility? Not the team's — yours.
A
Action: What did you do? Be specific — what did you investigate, decide, build, change? This is 60% of the story.
R
Result: Quantify if possible. Latency reduced by X%. Incidents reduced from N/month to M. Cost saved Y. If no numbers, describe the outcome clearly.
L
Learning: What would you do differently? This signals maturity. One sentence is enough.
